Diverse And Rich Range Of Subjects

Choosing subjects electives can be a daunting process, especially when Dominic College has such a rich range of subjects from which to choose.

Students should prepare carefully for choosing subjects by taking time to reflect on themselves, their interests and especially passions and dreams.

Ms Beth Giligan
Dominic College Principle
