How To Choose Year 8 Electives

Year 8 students will be studying 4 electives in 2021. Each student will need to choose 4 preferences and have 3 reserves.

Preference 1 needs to be from one of the Technology subjects so students will choose either Design in Metal, Design in Wood, Food and Textiles Technology or Digital Technologies.

Preference 2 will be from the Arts so that can be any one of Dance, Drama, Music or Visual Arts.

You will have access to all other options for your 3rd and 4th preferences with a choice of 3 reserve options.
Please remember to place you reserves in the order of preference.
