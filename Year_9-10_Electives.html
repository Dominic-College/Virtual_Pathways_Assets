How To Choose Year 9/10 Electives

For students moving into Years 9 and 10 next year, they will be studying 3 electives in 2021. Each student should choose 3 preferences and 2 reserves in order of preference.

Year 9 and 10 students require 2 reserves in the event that an elective does not run or if numbers do not permit.
Select your reserves in order of preference as well.

While we will make every effort to ensure that students are placed in their top preferences, sometimes we are unable to offer the subject or we have limited spaces available. This makes it important for students to ensure they carefully think about their preferences as well as the subjects they place on reserve.
